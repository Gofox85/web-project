About

Meenakshi Chaudhary is an Indian actress and former beauty pageant titleholder known for her work in the Telugu and Tamil film industries. Born in Panchkula, Haryana, she comes from a military family, and her late father was a colonel in the Indian Army. Before pursuing acting, Chaudhary earned a Bachelor's Degree in Dental Surgery (BDS) from the National Dental College in Punjab. She first gained national prominence in 2018 when she was crowned the first runner-up at the Femina Miss India pageant and later represented India at Miss Grand International 2018, where she also finished as the first runner-up. She began her acting career with roles in a web series and a Hindi film before making her lead debut in Telugu cinema with Ichata Vahanamulu Niluparadu in 2021. After establishing herself in Telugu films like HIT: The Second Case (2022) and Lucky Baskhar (2024), she made her Tamil debut in the 2023 psychological thriller Kolai, and has since appeared in other Tamil films, including Singapore Saloon and The Greatest of All Time. With a growing portfolio in South Indian cinema, Chaudhary is solidifying her position as a versatile and rising pan-India actress.
