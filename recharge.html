About

The annual national-level cultural festival "Recharge" is a prominent event hosted by the related Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC). This multi-day fest in Chennai attracts thousands of students from various colleges and features a wide range of competitions, music, dance performances, and star-studded pro-shows. In recent editions, "Recharge" has featured celebrity guests and well-known artists, demonstrating REC's commitment to providing a vibrant platform for student talent and entertainment.

From March 13 to 15, 2025, Rajalakshmi Engineering College hosted its annual intercollegiate cultural festival, Recharge 2025, which featured a wide array of celebrity appearances and performances. The event kicked off with a vibrant Ethnic Day celebration, followed by appearances from notable guests, including Tamil film director Adhik Ravichandran and actress Meenakshi Chaudhary. Highlights included an electrifying pro show by HipHop Tamizha, a special performance by singer Sai Abhyankkar, and a breathtaking display of acrobatics and dance from the America's Got Talent winners, The V Unbeatable. The high-energy festival provided a memorable experience for both students and attendees
